PRIME AUTHORITY – Official Organization Rules

These rules are mandatory for every member, staff, and team representative of Prime Authority.

# Rule Details
1 Professional Behaviour Har member, staff aur teammates ki respect karega. Toxicity, abuse, hate speech ya unnecessary drama allowed nahi hai.
2 Attendance & Punctuality Scrims, practice aur tournaments mein time par present rehna mandatory hai. Late ya absent hone par pehle inform karna hoga.
3 Activity Long inactivity bina notice ke allowed nahi hai. Agar break chahiye ho to management ko pehle batana hoga.
4 Team Discipline IGL aur management ke decisions ka respect karna hoga. Team ke andar arguments ya fights public nahi karni hain.
5 Fair Play Hacks, cheats, scripts, exploits ya third-party tools ka use strictly prohibited hai.
6 Confidentiality Team strategies, trial plans, internal discussions aur management information leak karna mana hai.
7 Social Media Organization ko represent karte waqt professional behaviour maintain karein. Fake announcements ya controversies create nahi karni.
8 Recruitment & Trials Selection sirf performance, discipline aur attitude ke basis par hoga. Fake information dene par application reject ho sakti hai.
9 Streaming & Content Official tournaments ke rules follow karne honge. Sponsor obligations aur branding guidelines ka respect karna hoga.
10 Leaving the Organization Organization leave karne se pehle management ko inform karna mandatory hai. Internal assets ya confidential information leak nahi ki jayegi.
11 Punishment System 1st Violation: Verbal Warning
2nd Violation: Written Warning
3rd Violation: Suspension
Serious Misconduct: Direct Permanent Removal
12 Management Rights Prime Authority management kisi bhi member ko warning, suspension ya removal dene ka adhikar rakhti hai.
